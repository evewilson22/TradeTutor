Time: 30 min | Tools Needed: Socket Wrench, Gloves | Difficulty: Easy

Step-by-step Instructions:

  • Open the hood and locate the battery.
  • Remove the cover from the battery (if there is one).
  • Use a socket wrench to loosen the bolt on the negative terminal.
  • Remove the negative cable and set it aside safely.
  • Repeat for the positive cable.
  • Remove the bolt securing the battery hold-down clamp.
  • Carefully lift out the old battery.
  • Place the new battery into the tray.
  • Reinstall the hold-down clamp and tighten the bolt.
  • Connect the positive cable first, then the negative cable.
  • Tighten both cable connections and replace the battery cover.
  • Close the hood and start the vehicle to ensure everything is working properly.
